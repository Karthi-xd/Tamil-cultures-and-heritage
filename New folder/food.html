Rice

Rice is a staple food in Tamil Nadu and plays a central role in the state's cuisine and culture

Popular Rice Dishes Sambar Rice: A comforting dish made by mixing rice with sambar, a lentil-based vegetable stew. Rasam Rice: Rice mixed with rasam, a tangy and spicy soup made with tamarind and tomatoes. Curd Rice: A cooling dish made by mixing rice with yogurt, often seasoned with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
