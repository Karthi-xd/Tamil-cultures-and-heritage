Tamil Cultures And Heritage



The Land Of Temples

Tamil culture is rich and fascinating, deeply rooted in traditions that have been preserved and celebrated for thousands of years. Originating in the Tamil Nadu state and parts of Sri Lanka, it's known for its distinctive language, literature, art, music, and dance forms. Here are a few highlights of Tamil culture:

Cultures

Bharatanatyam: One of the oldest classical dance forms in India, Bharatanatyam combines expressive movements and intricate footwork to tell stories from Hindu mythology.
